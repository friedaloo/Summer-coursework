Upcomming Events



Paris Photo

Nov 13-16, 2025

Description :

World's largest international photography fair. Celebrating its 28th edition in Grand Palais, Paris on 13-16 November 2025, this fair continues to bridge the historic and the contemporary, offering a dynamic, thought-provoking platform for the medium. The 2025 fair will feature approximately 224 exhibitors, comprising 183 galleries and 41 publishers from 33 countries, including over 60 first-time participants. Ticketing range form 26 Euro to 450 Euro depending on the access level. The fair is structured into five key sectors:
* Main (with the immersive Prisms projects).
* Emergence (solo projects from young galleries/artists).
* Digital (cutting-edge digital and AI-based photography).
* Voices (highlighting curated contemporary themes).
* Book (publishing and photobook showcases).

ART X Lagos 2025

Nov 6-9, 2026

Description :

ART X Lagos returns for its milestone tenth edition as the premier contemporary art fair in West Africa. The fair has grown into a global cultural catalyst—featuring galleries and artists from over 70 countries and drawing visitors from 170 nations. This event is organized to forges cross continental connections, helps shape the dialogue around African art, and consistently features heads and voices like El Anatsui. The key highlights of this events is theme, special projects, live music and talks.

15th Shanghai Biennale

Nov 7, 2025- April 26, 2026

Description :

It is one is huge event that takes place in Shanghai China. The theme of this event is Does the Flower Hear the Bee? which means sensory communication between humans, art, and nature. This event focus on interspecies intelligence, sensory experience, cultural memory, craft, Indigenous perspectives, AI & tech.

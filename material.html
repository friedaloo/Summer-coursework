Material used in Art

Material are very important in the art, you see tha art doesn't exit without the use of the material. Material are like the bridge in the art it connects artist vision to the real world from which other can also see, touch and feel. Without material the art would be only inside the artist inner vision. Different material have their own uses like oil paints are used for softness and depth and marble shows the strong sign. Not only that but also material are used for the art strength and durablity like in the ancient times artist used marble for the durablity and strength of the art. While in painting they use oil so that the painting will glow for longer time. Sometimes, material are used for symbolism like use of bronze shows the strength, wood feel organic and human while the gold shows the rich and power.
